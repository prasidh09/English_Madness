English Madness is an app that's a study aid providing anagrams, cliché, metaphors, palindromes. pleonasms, quotes and GRE words. It includes the top 500 GRE wordlist and the ease to share the words learnt. The app is compatible with all versions of windows 8.

Features

• Learn anagrams, cliches, metaphors, palindromes, pleonasms
• Kick start your GRE preparation
• Share your favourites with friends using the charms
• Enhance your knowledge with quotes
• Compatible for all Windows 8 devices
